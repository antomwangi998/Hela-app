    Why SACCO Members Build Wealth Faster Than Bank Customers
    Research by the Kenya Union of Savings & Credit Cooperatives (KUSCCO) shows that SACCO members accumulate 3× more wealth over a 10-year period compared to customers who only use commercial banks. The reasons are clear: higher savings interest rates, access to affordable credit at 1–1.5% per month vs banks' 2–3%, dividend income, and most importantly — the discipline enforced by monthly contribution targets.

    At HELA SMART SACCO, your savings earn 8% per annum while a typical bank pays 0.5–2%. Your loan rate of 1.5% per month reducing balance is far cheaper than mobile loan apps that charge 6–15% per month. This difference, compounded over years, creates enormous wealth for committed SACCO members.

    💡 Daily Money Tips
    💰 Tip #1: Automate your savings. Set a standing order on M-Pesa to deposit to your SACCO on salary day before you spend anything.
    📱 Tip #2: Delete mobile loan apps (Tala, Branch, Fuliza). SACCO loans at 1.5%/month are 4–10× cheaper.
    🎯 Tip #3: Use the 48-hour rule. Wait 2 days before any purchase over KSh 2,000. Most impulse buys feel unnecessary by then.
    📊 Tip #4: Track every shilling for 30 days. Most people are shocked where their money actually goes.
    🏠 Tip #5: Rent should not exceed 30% of your take-home pay. If it does, that's the first thing to fix.
    💳 Tip #6: Never borrow to fund a party, holiday, or clothing. These depreciate instantly. Borrow only for assets.
    📈 Tip #7: Invest in yourself first — skills and education are the highest ROI investment you will ever make.
